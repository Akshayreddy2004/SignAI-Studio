How SignAI Works

An inside look at the full-stack engineering, machine learning pipelines, and browser computer vision powering this application.

Deep Learning Hand Gesture Pipeline

For real-time Sign Language to Speech translation, we leverage **Google MediaPipe Hands** to extract 21 3D hand landmark coordinates (wrist, thumb, index, middle, ring, and pinky joints) at up to 30 FPS.

The normalized landmark vectors are processed through a custom-trained **TensorFlow Lite (TFLite) Neural Network** (`keypoint_classifier.tflite`) that classifies gestures with over 95% accuracy. To eliminate server latency, our upgraded studio executes this pipeline directly on the client browser canvas!

Django Full-Stack Backend

Powered by Python and Django 4+, our backend manages secure user authentication, session state, and an ORM-based `TranslationHistory` audit log that records every audio and gesture translation in real-time.

Web Speech Synthesis & Recognition

By utilizing HTML5 Web Speech APIs (`webkitSpeechRecognition` and `window.speechSynthesis`), we achieve zero-latency voice transcription and natural speech output without requiring native OS audio dependencies.
